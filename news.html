Starting Up the Organization

by Admin

Posted on August 3, 2016 at 9:00 PM

Do you want to get involved? Contact us to learn more about leadership opportunities in our organization. We need you!

We are looking to fill vacancies in our leadership team. The positions that are available are Vice President, Treasurer, and Secretary. Visit our 'Contact' page to let us know you are interested.

Brief descriptions of available positions:

Vice President

  • Maintain appropriate Organization documentation

  • Advertises meetings/events (via flyers, USD calendar events, etc.)

  • Recruits new Members

  • Facilitates election of Leaders

  • Handles all correspondence of the Organization

Treasurer

  • Maintains records of Organization finances

  • Prepares budget

  • Responsible for reimbursement protocol

  • Coordinates fundraising efforts

Secretary

  • Maintains a record of all members and activities of the Organization

  • Prepares an agenda with the President for all meetings

  • Keeps and distributes minutes of each meeting of the Organization

  • Obtains appropriate facilities for Organization activities
